Walks

Shoreditch Street Art Tours

www.shoreditchstreetarttours.co.uk

Pay what you want

Street artists from London’s east end and the UK as well as international artists from all over Europe and the rest of the World leave an ever changing kaleidoscope of energy and creativity on the walls of Shoreditch.


Market and a picnic in Victoria Park

Click for the map

One of our favourite walks. The map will take you through the heart of De Beauvoir, and straight to the canals. If here on a Saturday, we recommend getting off the canals at Broadway Market/London Fields and buying something yummy to eat before continuing on to Victory park.
